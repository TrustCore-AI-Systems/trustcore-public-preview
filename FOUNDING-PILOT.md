# Founding Pilot Program

The TrustCore Founding Pilot is an invitation‑only program for organizations that wish to shape and evaluate our pre‑execution control platform before it becomes generally available.  By joining the pilot, partners gain early access to features, priority support, and an opportunity to influence product direction.

## Objectives

* **Validate real‑world use cases.** Work with pilot partners to integrate the TrustCore gate into production or staging environments and demonstrate control before consequence.
* **Collect feedback.** Gather insights on policy definition, user experience, and integration challenges to refine our platform.
* **Ensure compliance.** Collaborate with partners’ legal, risk, and compliance teams to meet regulatory requirements and produce audit‑ready artifacts.

## Eligibility

We are initially targeting organizations that:

* Operate in regulated or high‑impact domains (e.g., finance, legal, healthcare, critical infrastructure).
* Employ AI agents that trigger external actions (payment execution, document filing, system modifications, communications).
* Have an internal identity and policy management system that can provide anchors (who, where, what, urgency, consent).
* Are willing to commit resources for integration and feedback during the pilot period.

If your organization does not fit this profile but you believe TrustCore could be valuable, please reach out—exceptions may be possible.

## What to Expect

* **Dedicated support.** Our engineering and governance teams will work closely with you to deploy the gate and integrate it into your workflows.
* **Private SDKs and APIs.** Pilot participants receive early versions of our client libraries and policy engine before public release.
* **Governance workshops.** We will host sessions with your teams to map policies, risk postures, and supervision requirements to the TrustCore model.
* **Feedback loops.** Regular check‑ins will ensure your insights directly influence upcoming features and priorities.

## Commitments

* **Confidentiality.** Pilot artifacts, code, and data remain confidential and must not be redistributed.  Participants may not publish benchmarks or performance data without prior consent.
* **Feedback.** Partners agree to provide structured feedback and collaborate on issue resolution.
* **Security.** Participants must follow the guidelines in our [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) and report any vulnerabilities privately.

## How to Apply

To express interest in the Founding Pilot, please email **pilot@trustcore.ai** with the following:

1. A brief description of your organization and industry.
2. A summary of the AI‑driven workflows you intend to govern with TrustCore.
3. Contact details for your technical lead and compliance lead.

We review pilot requests on a rolling basis and will contact selected partners for an initial discovery call.  Thank you for helping us build the future of **runtime governance**.
