Control Before Consequence

TrustCore builds a pre‑execution control layer for AI systems. We ensure that AI‑driven actions are governed, auditable, and subject to human oversight before they affect real‑world systems.

  • Runtime governance: Policies are enforced at execution time, not just during development.
  • Human approval boundary: High‑risk actions require explicit authorization. YES is not SEND.
  • Audit trail: Every approve, refuse, or supervised override produces a sealed record.
  • Vendor‑neutral design: Works with various AI frameworks and infrastructure.
